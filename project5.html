Baseline Survey & Economic Summit Support

Project Overview

This project conducted a socio-economic baseline survey and prepared for a Bougainville Economic and Investment Summit. It aims to inform policy, guide strategic planning, and support investment in Bougainville.

Key Components

  • Socio-economic baseline survey to gather data on communities, businesses, and services.
  • Preparations for the Bougainville Economic and Investment Summit to attract investors and partners.
  • Engagement with stakeholders to ensure data-driven planning and strategic recommendations.

Impact and Benefits

  • Informed strategic planning and investment decisions.
  • Improved understanding of economic and social conditions in Bougainville.
  • Strengthened capacity for evidence-based policymaking.

Project Partners

  • ABG: Led survey design and stakeholder engagement.
  • National Government of PNG: Provided support and coordination.
  • International partners: Supported funding and technical assistance.
