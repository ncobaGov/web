Peacebuilding & Inclusivity Projects

Project Overview

The Peacebuilding & Inclusivity Projects aim to facilitate inclusive dialogue, reconciliation, and reintegration of marginalized groups in Bougainville. These initiatives are part of the Sustaining Peace and Post-Referendum Support Projects supported by UNDP and other partners.

Key Components

  • Facilitating community dialogue and reconciliation programs.
  • Supporting reintegration of marginalized groups into social and political life.
  • Strengthening local governance and inclusive decision-making.

Impact and Benefits

  • Promotes peace and stability in post-referendum Bougainville.
  • Encourages participation of all communities, including women and youth.
  • Builds sustainable structures for ongoing peacebuilding efforts.

Project Partners

  • UNDP: Technical and financial support for program delivery.
  • ABG: Local implementation and oversight.
  • National Government of PNG: Support for coordination and policy alignment.
