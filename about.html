"From Peace to Autonomy, to Referendum and Beyond"

About NCOBA

After the signing of the Bougainville Peace Agreement and the enactment of the Organic Law on Peace Building in 2001, the National Executive Council (NEC), through a special NEC meeting No. 02/2007 and decision No. 10 of 2007 on 16 January 2007, established the Bougainville Peace and Restoration Office (BPRO). NCOBA is a constitutional office established to coordinate the implementation of autonomy, weapons disposal, and the referendum for Bougainville. Its key responsibility is to ensure effective coordination of the Bougainville Peace Agreement and post-referendum arrangements. NCOBA strengthens the relationship between the Government of PNG, the ABG, and the people of Bougainville.
