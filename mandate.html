Mandate

NCOBA was established by NEC at its special meeting No. 02/2007 and decision No. 10 of 2007 on the 16th January 2007. The NEC agreed to practical measures that would strengthen the national coordination in relation to the agreed agreement for Bougainville Autonomy, Restoration, and Development, and preparation for the guaranteed Referendum. The guaranteed Referendum has now been successfully concluded hence the shift of NCOBA’s mandate to cover the post-referendum arrangements. Consultation between the National Government and Autonomous Bougainville Government and the ratification by the National Parliament will now take place to further define and determine the political status of Bougainville.

Role & Responsibilities

NCOBA’s major role and responsibility is to effectively lead the coordination of the implementation of the Bougainville peace Agreement and the post referendum agreement on behalf of the National Government.
