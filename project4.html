UNDP Equipment Support (2021)

Project Overview

The UNDP Equipment Support project provided vehicles and ICT equipment to strengthen the operational capacity of NCOBA during the crucial post-referendum period. This support enhanced outreach, logistics, and administrative efficiency.

Key Components

  • Provision of vehicles to improve mobility for staff across Bougainville.
  • Delivery of ICT equipment including computers, printers, and communication tools.
  • Technical assistance to optimize use and maintenance of equipment.

Impact and Benefits

  • Enhanced operational capacity and outreach capabilities.
  • Improved communication and data management within NCOBA.
  • Increased efficiency in coordinating post-referendum activities.

Project Partners

  • UNDP: Provided technical and financial support.
  • ABG: Coordinated implementation at the local level.
  • National Government of PNG: Supported planning and logistics.
