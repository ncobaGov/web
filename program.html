"From Peace to Autonomy, to Referendum and Beyond"

Community Activities

We believe in the power of community and connection. Our diverse range of activities is designed to bring people together, foster learning, and promote well-being. From annual conferences to local workshops, there's something for everyone. These events provide valuable networking opportunities and a platform for sharing knowledge and experiences.

Our flagship events include the Annual General Meeting, where members can influence the direction of our organization, and various professional development seminars aimed at enhancing skills. We also host social gatherings and charity drives to strengthen community bonds and give back.

e-Participations

In today's digital world, we are committed to making our programs accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our e-Participation platforms allow you to engage with our content and community from the comfort of your home or office. Participate in live webinars, join discussion forums, and contribute to online polls and surveys.

These digital initiatives ensure that geographical barriers do not limit participation. We leverage technology to create inclusive and interactive experiences. Our webinars feature industry experts sharing insights on current trends, and all sessions are recorded and made available on-demand for our members.

Consultation Papers

Your voice matters. We regularly publish consultation papers to seek feedback from our members and stakeholders on key issues, proposed policies, and strategic directions. This collaborative approach ensures that our decisions are well-informed, transparent, and reflective of the community's needs and aspirations. We invite you to review the documents below and share your valuable input.
