Special Projects

Community Mapping Initiative

Volunteers and local leaders collaborated to collect geospatial data, which was then visualized using open-source mapping tools.

Youth Empowerment Program

Designed to equip young people with digital skills, this program offers workshops in coding, design, and entrepreneurship.

Participants receive mentorship from professionals and access to online learning platforms.

Graduates have gone on to launch startups and contribute to community tech hubs.

We aim to expand the program to rural schools and integrate it into national education policy.

This digital archive preserves oral histories, traditional art, and indigenous knowledge.

The archive is accessible online and used in schools to promote cultural pride.

Plans include interactive exhibits and mobile storytelling kiosks.
