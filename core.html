Our Core Values

Integrity

  • Practice professionalism, transparency, and best practices at workplace.
  • Compliant to established guidelines, systems, processes, and procedures.
  • Trust and value our clients, service providers, and the people of Bougainville and PNG.

Responsibility & Accountability

  • Deliver mandated tasks and duties effectively and efficiently.
  • Accountable for decisions, actions, and inactions.
  • Provide user-friendly services to clients, service providers, and stakeholders.

Relationship & Competence

  • Practice teamwork, collaboration, and cooperation within NCOBA.
  • Collaborate and cooperate with external government agencies and stakeholders.
  • Continuous improvement of skills, knowledge, and performance.
  • Strive for excellence and results.

Respect

  • Value and respect each staff member and clients.
  • Promote fairness and honesty at workplace.

Gender Equality & Social Inclusion

  • Embrace gender equality and social inclusion policy principles and applications at workplace.
