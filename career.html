Careers at NCOBA

Join a dedicated team at the heart of national policy and coordination. We seek skilled professionals committed to public service and the successful implementation of the Bougainville Peace Agreement.

Senior Policy Advisor

Analyze complex policies and provide high-level strategic advice on matters concerning Bougainville affairs.

View Details

Project Management Officer

Coordinate and manage key government projects and initiatives related to peace-building and development in the region.

View Details

Communications Officer

NCOBA Scholarship Program

We are committed to building future capacity. Our scholarship programs support outstanding students dedicated to contributing to the future of Bougainville and Papua New Guinea.

Peace & Development Scholarship

For students pursuing degrees in Political Science, Law, International Relations, or Development Studies.

Bougainville Future Leaders Scholarship
