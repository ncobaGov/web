Corporate Plan & Website Launch (2020–2025)

Project Overview

The National Coordination Office for Bougainville Affairs (NCOBA) launched its first-ever corporate plan covering 2020–2025 along with an official website. This project marked a major step in post-referendum planning, institutional strengthening, and public engagement.

Corporate Plan (2020–2025)

  • Launch & Leadership: Officially launched in Port Moresby by Minister Sir Puka Temu with NCOBA Acting Director John Anuma.
  • Purpose: Roadmap for post-referendum governance, aligning with PNG Vision 2050 and Medium Term Development Plans.
  • Institutional Significance: First formal plan since NCOBA’s establishment around 2010, guiding operations and coordination effectively.

Official Website Launch

  • Rationale: A modern portal for timely and unified messaging from the National Government and ABG.
  • Purpose: Central hub for information on NCOBA’s mission, values, leadership, governance, and updates.
  • Goals: Transparency, public awareness, and accurate information sharing to complement post-referendum planning.
